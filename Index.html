Build Philosophy
You are not the main damage dealer. Your job is to jump in first, buff your allies, create chaos with CC, and then enable your team to capitalise while you apply sustained bleed pressure. You do lose some raw damage compared to other builds — that is the tradeoff for team impact and control. This build rewards coordination and punishes solo play.

Bleeds + Anti-Heal

Defensives — Use As Needed
Unyielding Spirit
Grants Invulnerability (fully immune to incoming damage) for 3s via passive node. Breaks all Control effects on activation. Save for real danger.
Decisive Action
Instantly grants Resilience (CC immunity) for 5s and breaks all Control effects. Use when locked down.
Revitalizing Burst / Indomitable Resolve
Healing and fortitude. Sustain yourself through prolonged fights — manage them actively rather than panic-popping.

Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
  • Strong team buffing and ally empowerment
  • Powerful AoE engage combo
  • Hard to peel and lock down
  • Bleeds and anti-heal deny enemy sustain
Weaknesses
  • Damage output lower than dedicated DPS builds
  • Weaker at isolating and killing single targets
  • Relies on team synergy to get full value from abilities
When to Use This Build
Use When
  • Playing with a coordinated, communicating team
  • Team needs a frontline that enables follow-up
  • Running an engage-heavy composition
  • Fights where AoE control wins the round
Avoid When
  • Solo queuing without communication
  • Team lacks damage to follow your engage
  • Targets need to be isolated and burst individually
