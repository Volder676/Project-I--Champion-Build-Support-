Build Identity
Role
Frontline Enabler Ally Buffer
Playstyle Flow
Engage CC AoE Bleed
Best Environment
Coordinated team, group PvP, organised fights
Build Philosophy
You are not the main damage dealer. Your job is to jump in first, buff your allies, create chaos with CC, and then enable your team to capitalise while you apply sustained bleed pressure. You do lose some raw damage compared to other builds — that is the tradeoff for team impact and control. This build rewards coordination and punishes solo play.

Ability Reference
Rising Fury
Buff / Engage Trigger
Instantly gains Precision and Empower. Always activate before engaging — this powers both your burst and your allies' follow-up damage.
Precision: +20% crit · 6s Empower: +1% dmg ×30 · 6s Cost: 25 Fury
Prelude → Cataclysm
Engage / CC — 2-Part Ability
Prelude launches you forward, slashing on landing and Hindering enemies. Cataclysm follows — sunder the ground for heavy AoE damage and a 2s Stun. Note: Cataclysm adds 10s to Prelude's cooldown.
Prelude: 1,710 DMG · Hinder 4s Cataclysm: 2,040 DMG · Stun 2s
Rupture
CC / Setup
Ruptures ground in a line, dealing damage, Stunning enemies for 2s, and granting you Celerity (+25% move speed for 6s). Use after landing to extend CC uptime on the cluster.
1,995 DMG · Stun 2s · Range 12m Celerity: +25% speed · 6s
Channeled Blades
Main AoE Damage
Conjures spinning blades for 6 ticks of AoE damage in a 4.8m radius. Grants Accelerated while channeling (+50% move speed, immune to Impairment). Generates 10 Fury per tick.
1,020 DMG × 6 ticks Accelerated: move immune +10 Fury/tick
Onslaught
Burst Follow-Up
Unleashes rage dealing AoE damage and generating 20 Fury/sec for 8s. Cooldown reduced by 1s per 60 Fury spent. Use immediately after Channeled Blades to extend the pressure window.
1,080 DMG (scales down per target) +20 Fury/sec · 8s Cost: 50 Fury
Bull's Roar
Reset / Damage
AoE damage roar that grants Haste (−25% activation time) for 3s. Used in Round 2 to reset into a second Channeled Blades window via passive node.
1,965 DMG · Radius 5.6m Haste: −25% activation · 3s
Unyielding Spirit
Defensive / Immortality
Become immortal for 4s (HP cannot drop below 1). Breaks all Control effects on activation. On expiry, heals 500 per hit dealt during the buff (max 3000 HP). Save for real danger.
Immortal · 4s Heal 500/hit · Max 3000 Breaks CC on use
Decisive Action
CC Break / Immunity
Instantly grants Resilience (full CC immunity) for 5s and breaks all Control effects on activation. Use when you or your party are locked down.
Resilience: CC immune · 5s Breaks CC on use Cost: 25 Fury
Mobility Skills — Heroic Leap · Savage Charge
Gap Close / Escape
Used to close distance fast during the engage or to escape when overextended. Chain Savage Charge (rank IV — highest priority) with Heroic Leap to land on the target cluster. Can also be used reactively to disengage from dangerous positions if your defensive cooldowns are spent.
Gash · Hemorrhage · Lacerate
Sustained Pressure / Anti-Heal
Bleed and anti-heal pressure applied in Round 2 after CC wears off. Never apply these while targets have CC immunity — wait until they are free and trying to recover. This keeps constant pressure going and denies healing.
Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
  • Strong team buffing and ally empowerment
  • Powerful AoE engage combo
  • Hard to peel and lock down
  • Bleeds and anti-heal deny enemy sustain
Weaknesses
  • Damage output lower than dedicated DPS builds
  • Weaker at isolating and killing single targets
  • Relies on team synergy to get full value from abilities
When to Use This Build
Use When
  • Playing with a coordinated, communicating team
  • Team needs a frontline that enables follow-up
  • Running an engage-heavy composition
  • Fights where AoE control wins the round
Avoid When
  • Solo queuing without communication
  • Team lacks damage to follow your engage
  • Targets need to be isolated and burst individually
